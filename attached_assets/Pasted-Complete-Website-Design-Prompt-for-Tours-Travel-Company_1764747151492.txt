Complete Website Design Prompt for Tours & Travel Company (No Login Required)
1. VISUAL DESIGN & UI AESTHETICS
Color Palette:

Primary: Deep teal/ocean blue (#0A7E8C) - trust and adventure
Secondary: Warm sunset orange (#FF6B35) - energy and excitement
Accent: Soft gold (#F4A261) for CTAs and highlights
Neutrals: Clean whites, soft grays, deep charcoal for text
Use vibrant photography overlays with subtle gradients

Typography:

Headings: Modern sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins) - bold, adventurous
Body: Clean, readable (Inter, Open Sans)
Mix weights creatively: thin for elegance, bold for impact

Design Style:

Modern, immersive with full-bleed imagery
Glassmorphism effects on cards and overlays
Smooth parallax scrolling
Micro-animations on hover and scroll
Mobile-first responsive design
